ACM Teach LA

making coding
accessible. fun. empowering. approachable. easy.

A subcommittee of UCLA ACM, ACM Teach LA pairs UCLA undergraduates with schools in Los Angeles to provide free coding classes for students. Our goal is to empower all students with the ability to code and make a difference.

Learn More

How does it work?

Classes typically run for a quarter and meet once a week. Each class begins with a review before diving into new material. One teacher leads the lesson while others walk around the room to help students. Teachers are expected to attend a weekly meeting to discuss curriculum for the following week’s lesson. We strongly emphasize pedagogy training and hold seminars to teach how to teach.
